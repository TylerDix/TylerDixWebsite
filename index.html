About

I can summarize good design in one word: thoughtful. Most designs today are overly designed, careless, and sloppy. Before I create anything, I carefully think about three things. Audience, purpose, and scope. Every single design decision I make after this must serve these three things. After I determine these objectives, the fun begins! I absolutely love what I do and if I didn’t need to support myself, I’d do it all for free.
